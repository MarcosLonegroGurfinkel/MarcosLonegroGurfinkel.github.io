Globant


I worked as a summer intern in February 2018 in Globant's office at Buenos Aires. Globant was one of Argentina's five unicorns (startups valued at more than $1 bn) and is now a major player in the software industry.

I was part of the Internet of Things department, which looked at ways to fuse hardware and software into products for clients. While I worked there, the team was exploring the creation of a wristband that could be used to pay for gas in gastations and a way to send the results of a 'metegol' match to players' phones.

It was a great learning experience in the domains of Arduino development, the process of design and production using 3D printers and client interaction. I even learned to wield chips!
